# Competitor Teardown — Acme Analytics (Q2 2026)

Internal research memo for the research assistant practice.

## Pricing

- **Starter:** $29/user/month — up to 5 seats, basic dashboards
- **Growth:** $79/user/month — SSO, CSV export, API access
- **Enterprise:** custom — dedicated support, SAML, audit logs

## Product gaps (vs us)

- **Dark mode:** shipped in Acme v4.2 (April 2026)
- **Real-time collaboration:** not available on Starter tier
- **Mobile app:** iOS only; Android planned Q4 2026

## Recent launches

- **AI summary widget** (March 2026) — auto-generates weekly digest emails
- **Slack integration** (May 2026) — read-only alerts, no two-way sync yet

## Takeaway

Acme leads on dark mode and mobile polish; we win on pricing flexibility for teams under 20 seats.
